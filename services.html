Frequently Asked Questions

How much do your services cost?

We offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your specific situation.

How long does debt review take?

The process typically takes 30-60 days from application to implementation of reduced payments.

Will debt review affect my credit score?

Debt review is designed to help you manage debt responsibly. While it appears on your credit report, it shows you're taking positive action.

Can I get credit while in debt review?

Access to new credit may be limited during debt review, but this is temporary and your credit will improve once you complete the program.

What documents do I need?

You'll need ID, proof of income, bank statements, and details of all your debts and creditors.

Is debt mediation legally binding?

Yes, mediated agreements are legally binding contracts that both parties must adhere to.
