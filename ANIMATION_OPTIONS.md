# Page Title Animation Options

Choose your preferred animation style for page titles:

## 1. **Typewriter Effect**
   - Text appears character by character like typing
   - Includes a blinking cursor
   - Classic, professional look

## 2. **Letter-by-Letter Pop**
   - Each letter appears one by one with a bounce/pop effect
   - Sequential reveal with slight delay between letters
   - Playful and dynamic

## 3. **Fade In with Glow**
   - Text fades in smoothly
   - Glowing effect that pulses or expands
   - Elegant and modern

## 4. **Slide In from Left/Right**
   - Text slides in from the side
   - Can include rotation or scale effects
   - Clean and professional

## 5. **Split Reveal (Current)**
   - Text comes from both sides and meets in the middle
   - 3D rotation effect
   - Bold and dramatic

## 6. **Wave/Stagger Effect**
   - Letters appear in a wave pattern
   - Creates a flowing, rhythmic motion
   - Smooth and elegant

## 7. **Zoom In with Blur**
   - Text starts large and blurred, zooms in to focus
   - Or starts small and zooms out
   - Modern and cinematic

## 8. **Flip/3D Rotate**
   - Text flips or rotates in 3D space
   - Can flip horizontally or vertically
   - Eye-catching and dynamic

## 9. **Gradient Reveal**
   - Text is revealed by a gradient mask moving across
   - Creates a "wipe" effect
   - Smooth and polished

## 10. **Bounce In**
   - Text bounces into place with elastic effect
   - Can include multiple bounces
   - Energetic and fun

## 11. **Glitch Effect**
   - Text appears with a digital glitch/distortion effect
   - Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
   - Bold and attention-grabbing

## 12. **Morph/Transform**
   - Text morphs from one shape or style to another
   - Smooth transformation effect
   - Unique and creative

## 13. **Particle/Sparkle Reveal**
   - Text appears with particle effects or sparkles
   - Magical, premium feel
   - Elegant and sophisticated

## 14. **Underline Draw**
   - Text fades in, then underline draws from left to right
   - Clean and minimal
   - Professional and classic

## 15. **Scale with Rotation**
   - Text scales up while rotating slightly
   - Smooth entrance with depth
   - Modern and refined

## 16. **Slide Up with Fade**
   - Text slides up from below while fading in
   - Simple and elegant
   - Professional standard

## 17. **Ripple Effect**
   - Text appears with a ripple/wave effect spreading outward
   - Water-like animation
   - Smooth and calming

## 18. **Neon Glow Pulse**
   - Text appears with a neon-style glow that pulses
   - Modern, tech aesthetic
   - Bold and vibrant

## 19. **Letter Scatter & Assemble**
   - Letters start scattered and assemble into the title
   - Dynamic and playful
   - Eye-catching

## 20. **Shimmer/Shine Sweep**
   - Text fades in, then a shine sweeps across repeatedly
   - Premium, polished look
   - Elegant and refined
